VIOLINIST
Scottish violinist, Alexandra Merbecks, graduated from the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland with a Master of Arts in Violin Performance. Now based in Milan, Alexandra is developing an international career.

As a soloist, Alexandra has performed the Bruch Violin Concerto with the Edrom Casals Ensemble and Mozart Violin Concerto No.3 with the Edinburgh Napier Chamber Orchestra. Alexandra has given many notable recitals, including an evening of salon music at the Scottish Arts Club.

Chamber music highlights include a tour to The Netherlands with the Forth Trio, with one performance recorded live on local television. The ensemble also appeared at the Edinburgh International Festival. Alexandra was awarded Finalist in the Edinburgh Quartet Apprentice Competition.

TEACHING

Alexandra is a dedicated and attentive educator, with over five years of violin instrumental and coaching experience in both individual and group settings. With an imaginative and intelligent approach to teaching, Alexandra ensures that every child develops a solid technical foundation all the while fostering a fun and exploratory relationship with their violin.

Alexandra enjoys developing her teaching skills to adapt the teaching method to suit the student. She is a qualified Colourstrings Violin instructor, has experience in teaching with the Kodály method and is currently an Instrumental Ambassador for the Benedetti Foundation .

Alexandra holds a certificate in the Essentials of Performing Arts Medicine (Arts Educator) from the Performing Arts Medicine Association .
